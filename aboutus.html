Who We Are

Dream Company was founded a 1 year back with a dream to bring up India on Destination Weddings Map. We fortunately acted as pioneer into this specific niche and got an opportunity to promote exotic locations like Rajasthan, Goa and Kerala worldwide to project them as lovely locations to get married. We evolved the concept of Destination Weddings in India and got much required international publicity to gain that momentum.

Over the years, we have assisted more than 400 overseas couples to plan their dream day in India and we have also worked with numerous locations and venues to help them prepared as “wedding friendly” option. Our strength is a dedicated team of experienced wedding planners and coordinators, travel and tourism professionals. Our experience working with overseas couples play a vital role in easing out all the stress in planning a wedding sitting continents away!

“Together is a wonderful place to be”. You have held each others’ hand through the ups and downs. We, at Dream Team, salute your beloved and you! We are always happy to step in and immortalize your love with a celebration that would create another memory you can fondly share together.
